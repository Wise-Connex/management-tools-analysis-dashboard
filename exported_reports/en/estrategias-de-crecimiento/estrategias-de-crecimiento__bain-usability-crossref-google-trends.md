Ignoring this drop in usability risks executive failure and the waste of organizational capital.

2.  **Contextual Customization:** Given the wide range in usability scores, it is imperative to avoid generic solutions. The tool must be tailored to align with the specific culture, industry, and digital maturity of the company, using comparative metrics as a benchmark.
3.  **Pilot Deployment and Iteration:** Begin with pilot projects in business units with high visibility but controlled risk. The objective should be to achieve early wins that validate the tool's utility, generating internal evidence to counteract the global trend of decreasing usability. Constant feedback will allow for real-time strategy adjustments.
