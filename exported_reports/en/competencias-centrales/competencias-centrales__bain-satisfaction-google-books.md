2. **Aggressive Resource Stratification:** Divest from business units and operational activities that do not directly feed the 2-3 identified core competencies. Redirect this freed-up capital and talent toward deepening the primary competitive moat.
3. **Establishment of a Strategic Watch:** Implement a permanent executive task force dedicated to evaluating the remaining lifespan of current core competencies against emerging technological and macro-economic trends. This ensures the framework functions as an adaptive system rather than a static, restrictive bureaucratic anchor.
