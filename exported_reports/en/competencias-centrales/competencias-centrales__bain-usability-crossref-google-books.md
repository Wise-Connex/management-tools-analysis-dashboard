Leadership must pivot from viewing core competencies as permanent structural assets to treating them as dynamic, adaptable capabilities that must evolve continuously.

**Adoption and Implementation Recommendations**

To reverse the trend of declining usability and bridge the theory-practice gap, organizations are strongly advised to modernize their adoption of the Competencias Centrales framework:

*   **Dynamic Reframing:** Shift the organizational definition of core competencies from fixed operational strengths to dynamic capabilities that can be rapidly reconfigured in response to market disruptions.
