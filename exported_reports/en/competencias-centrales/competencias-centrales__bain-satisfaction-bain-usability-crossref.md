2. **Cross-Functional Integration:** Leverage the growing body of Crossref literature to form cross-functional teams that include data analysts and strategy experts, ensuring that competency identification is both empirically grounded and operationally relevant.
3. **Dynamic Auditing:** Establish a continuous monitoring system for identified core competencies. Given the volatility of modern markets, competencies must be audited semi-annually to prevent the onset of core rigidities.

In conclusion, the Competencias Centrales framework remains a highly satisfying and potent strategic instrument. However, to ensure its survival and relevance beyond 2023, practitioners must rigorously modernize its application, actively mitigating the usability decline through agile, data-informed implementation strategies.
