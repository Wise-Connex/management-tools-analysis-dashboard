In conclusion, Planificación Estratégica remains a cornerstone of organizational management. However, its survival and increasing relevance depend entirely on its evolution. Organizations must transition from static, compliance-driven models to dynamic, data-informed, and agile strategic frameworks to secure a sustainable competitive advantage.
