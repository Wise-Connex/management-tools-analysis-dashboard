This disparity suggests a profound theory-practice gap. Academic research and published literature are expanding prolifically, frequently focusing on highly structured, complex methodologies. To optimize the adoption and ROI of "Gestión del Cambio", we recommend organizations pivot toward agile, micro-change strategies rather than massive, monolithic transformations:
