Organizations should invest in continuous internal training programs focused on translating dense academic literature into pragmatic, actionable frameworks tailored to their specific corporate culture.
3.  **Agile Integration:** The decreasing trend in search volume does not signal obsolescence; rather, it points toward an evolution toward more agile, embedded practices. Adoption strategies should pivot from heavy, bureaucratic models to leaner, more dynamic frameworks that facilitate faster adaptation.

In conclusion, "Gestión del Cambio" remains a highly relevant discipline.
