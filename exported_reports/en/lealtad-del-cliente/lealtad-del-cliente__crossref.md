The analysis reveals a decreasing trend in academic production regarding Customer Loyalty, suggesting that the field may be reaching a level of theoretical maturity. Customer loyalty research has historically demonstrated a relatively weak connection between academic rigor and business applicability. This analysis suggests that:

- Traditional loyalty models are being challenged by new perspectives centered on customer experience.

- Technological integration in loyalty programs has evolved more rapidly than the theoretical understanding of its impacts.
