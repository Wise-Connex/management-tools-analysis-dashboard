This analysis examines the management tool **Customer Loyalty** (Lealtad del Cliente) through a multi-source study spanning over seven decades, from 1950 to 2023. From a theoretical perspective, the gap between popular practice and academia indicates a **concept maturation**. Researchers delve into complex loyalty models (affective, cognitive, behavioral), while in practice, tools have become more sophisticated with big data and artificial intelligence.

### Adoption Recommendations

For executives and organizational leaders:

1. **Do not underestimate the tool**: The decline in searches is misleading; loyalty is more relevant than ever under more specific terms.
2. **Invest in advanced training**: Train teams in evidence-based models that transcend basic points programs.
3. **Integrate into overall strategy**: Design predictive loyalty strategies that integrate customer behavior and emotion data.
4. **Monitor academic research**: Establish bridges between academia and practice to stay at the forefront of customer retention knowledge.

In conclusion, **Customer Loyalty** is experiencing a **quiet but robust evolution**. Organizations that recognize this maturity and leverage the growing body of knowledge will be better positioned to build lasting, profitable relationships in today's economy.
