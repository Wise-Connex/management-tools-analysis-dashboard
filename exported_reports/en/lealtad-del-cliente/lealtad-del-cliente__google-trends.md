**Adoption Recommendations**
For strategic planning and adoption, organizations should avoid over-indexing on traditional, siloed 'loyalty programs' that characterized the early 2000s. Instead, leadership teams are advised to:
1. **Modernize the Lexicon:** Recalibrate internal KPIs and strategic communications to reflect current paradigms, integrating loyalty metrics into comprehensive Customer Experience (CX) frameworks.

In conclusion, the decreasing search interest in 'Lealtad del Cliente' does not signify the death of customer retention, but rather its evolution into a complex, technology-driven operational standard. Executives must adapt their strategies to reflect this sophisticated reality.
