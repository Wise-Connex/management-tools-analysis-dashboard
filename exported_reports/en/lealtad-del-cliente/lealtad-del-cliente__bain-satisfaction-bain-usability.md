The most salient finding in this analysis is the significant divergence between two critical dimensions: **Usability** and **Satisfaction**. Conversely, the satisfaction metric, with a mean of 54.06 and higher variability (standard deviation of 35.22), proves that when organizations successfully navigate this complexity, the derived strategic value is substantial. In essence, the tool has become harder to master, but the payoff for that mastery has increased significantly.
