**Adoption Recommendations**
Based on the evidence, we recommend three strategic actions for organizational leaders. Second, bridge the theory-practice gap by actively leveraging current academic insights (reflected in Crossref's upward trend) to optimize processes against modern digital disruptions and supply chain volatility.
