4. **Integrate with Digital Transformation:** Modern BPR should not be conducted in a vacuum. Executives should leverage contemporary technologies (AI, cloud computing) as essential enablers to sustain the redesigned processes and maintain a continuous improvement loop.

In conclusion, Business Process Reengineering remains a potent and viable management tool.
