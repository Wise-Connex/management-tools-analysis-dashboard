**Implementation Strategy**
To mitigate risks and maximize return, a phased implementation strategy is recommended:
1.  **Data Audit:** Ensure the quality of historical sales data before implementing any algorithmic model.
2.  **Pilot Testing:** Implement the tool on a specific product line to calibrate the model and build stakeholder confidence.
3.  **Training:** Invest heavily in management team training to reduce variability in satisfaction and ensure proper interpretation of system recommendations.
