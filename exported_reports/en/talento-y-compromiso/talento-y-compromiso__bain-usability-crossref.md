**Adoption and Implementation Recommendations**
Based on the empirical evidence provided by the data, we recommend a highly cautious and selective approach to the adoption of "Talento y Compromiso":

1. **De-escalate Standalone Usage:** Organizations currently utilizing this tool should phase out its use as a primary, standalone diagnostic metric for workforce engagement.

3. **Prioritize Customization:** Given the high variance in usability success (ranging from 0.0 to 100.0 in the data), executives must mandate strict localization and customization of the framework to fit specific cultural and operational contexts before any rollout.
